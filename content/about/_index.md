Welcome to Hacking the Cloud! Hacking the cloud is a encyclopedia of attacks/tactics/techniques that offensive security professionals can use on their next cloud exploitation adventure.

All content on this site is created by volunteers. If you'd like to be one of them, you can contribute your knowledge by submitting a [Pull Request](https://github.com/Hacking-the-Cloud/hackingthe.cloud/pulls), contributing an article to your cloud of choice.
